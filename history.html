History & Kings

Karnataka's history is a rich tapestry woven by some of Southern India's greatest empires. For centuries, this land was the cradle of powerful dynasties that left an indelible mark on the region's art, architecture, and culture.

From the architectural marvels of the Hoysalas to the golden age of the Vijayanagara Empire, the legacy of these kingdoms can be seen today in the magnificent temples and majestic forts scattered across the state. Each dynasty contributed its unique vision, creating a historical landscape that is both diverse and profound.

The Hoysala Empire

Famed for their intricate temple architecture, with stunning examples at Belur, Halebidu, and Somanathapura.

The Vijayanagara Empire

Known for establishing the magnificent capital at Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and for fostering a golden age of art and literature.

The Chalukya Dynasty

Pioneers of a unique style of temple architecture, with their legacy visible in the cave temples of Badami and monuments at Aihole.
